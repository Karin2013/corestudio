What if google maps could provide
indoor mapping of places that are
being publicly visited?

This project is to create narratives and tell a story using different online platforms.
My story is about Jenny who found an exhibition that she was interested and came to visit it.
She had trouble finding the exact location of the exhibition, but she solved the problem
through Google maps and wrote a review after her journey.
